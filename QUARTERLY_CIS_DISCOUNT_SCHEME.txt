================================================================================
        SPECIAL CIS LINKED POST SALES DISCOUNT - QUARTERLY SCHEME (ZQIS)
                       (As Implemented in YRVG004_QAIS_EXECUTE)
================================================================================

1.  Special CIS Linked Post Sales Discount - Quarterly (ZQIS):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Quarter Periods covered:

    Q1 : April   01  to  June      30
    Q2 : July    01  to  September 30
    Q3 : October 01  to  December  31
    Q4 : January 01  to  March     31

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Month-wise Lifting Quantities mapped per Quarter:

    +----------+-------+-------+-------+-------------------+
    | Quarter  | M-1   | M-2   | M-3   | Fields Used       |
    +----------+-------+-------+-------+-------------------+
    | Q1       | APR   | MAY   | JUN   | m1  / m2  / m3    |
    | Q2       | JUL   | AUG   | SEP   | m4  / m5  / m6    |
    | Q3       | OCT   | NOV   | DEC   | m7  / m8  / m9    |
    | Q4       | JAN   | FEB   | MAR   | m10 / m11 / m12   |
    +----------+-------+-------+-------+-------------------+

    Fields per month:
      grp_lift_qty_mXX  = Group lifting quantity (all customers in group)
      ind_lift_qty_mXX  = Individual customer lifting quantity
      ind_elgl_qty_mXX  = Individual eligible quantity (after min/max cap)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2.  Discount Eligibility Criteria:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Sl.   Eligibility Condition               Eligible Quantity (Ind.)
    No.
    ---   ----------------------------------- --------------------------------
    i.    Group Lifting (BCQ) >= Min% of QCQ  Eligible for discount
          AND <= Max% of QCQ

    ii.   Group Lifting > Max% of QCQ         Capped at Max% of QCQ
                                              (pro-rated for individual)

    iii.  Group Lifting < Min% of QCQ         NOT eligible (qty = 0)
    ---   ----------------------------------- --------------------------------

    Where:
      QCQ (Quarterly Committed Qty) = sum of committed_qty_m1 + m2 + m3
                                      for the respective quarter
      BCQ (Bi-monthly Committed Qty) = as applicable per amendment
      Min% = wa_yrva_mstr_waiver-min_perc_qX  (default: 85%)
      Max% = wa_yrva_mstr_waiver-max_perc_qX  (default: 125%)

    P&D Sector Customers:
      If p_d_sector = 'X', the Max% cap is replaced by pd_perc_qX
      from master waiver table (yrva_mstr_waiver).
      If pd_perc_qX is initial, no upper cap is applied (open-ended).

    Special Waiver:
      If waiver_qty = 'Q1', the min_perc_q1 is reduced to 65%.
      Additional per-month waivers (yrva_qais_add_wv) are also checked.
      Revival failure (yrva_revival_fai) may override eligibility.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3.  Eligible Quantity Formula:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Total Group Lifting (BCQ) = grp_lift_qty_m1 + grp_lift_qty_m2
                                + grp_lift_qty_m3

    Individual Eligible Qty (per month):
      ind_elgl_qty_mX = ind_lift_qty_mX   (if BCQ within min-max band)

    If BCQ > Max Cap:
      ind_elgl_qty_mX = (ind_lift_qty_mX / grp_lift_qty_mX) * Max_Cap_Qty

    Total Eligible Qty (quarter):
      tot_elgl_qty = ind_elgl_qty_m1 + ind_elgl_qty_m2 + ind_elgl_qty_m3

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4.  Discount Rate Determination (Condition Type ZQIS):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Rate is fetched from SAP condition table:
      A350  (kappl='V', kschl='ZQIS', vkorg='5000')
      KONM  (scale-based rate table via knumh)

    Scale lookup (sorted DESCENDING by kstbm):
      Rate (w_kbetr) = kbetr where kstbm <= customer's QCQ (mou_qty)
      i.e., the highest scale bracket not exceeding the committed qty.

    Discount Amount:
      value = tot_elgl_qty * w_kbetr

    Note: If computed value is negative, absolute value is used.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5.  Quarter-4 (January to March) - Special Rules:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    i.   Customers with mou_begda >= 01-Feb-2024 are EXCLUDED from
         Q4 quarterly discount (lv_flag123 is set, record is skipped).

    ii.  Q4 months mapped as:
           January  = grp/ind_lift_qty_m10
           February = grp/ind_lift_qty_m11
           March    = grp/ind_lift_qty_m12

    iii. w_endda_prev = selection date - 1 (previous quarter end date)
         is computed to support prior-quarter data lookup.

    iv.  P&D sector: max_perc_q4 replaced by pd_perc_q4.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6.  Quarterly Discount ALV Output Columns:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Customer No.    | Customer Name   | Group Code (KVGR2)
    Sales Office    | Committed Qty   | Group Lifting (M1/M2/M3)
    Ind. Lifting    | Ind. Eligible   | Total Group Lifting
    Total Eligible  | Disc. Rate      | Remarks
    Sale Order No.  | Select (checkbox)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7.  Grade / Sector Applicability:
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    Grades applicable are configured in table YRVA_GRADE_CISD.
    S922 lifting records are filtered using lt_kondm (grade range).

    Sector ranges (from YRVA_PRS_GRADES):
      range_p  = P-sector grades  (indicator = 'P')
      range_r  = R-sector grades  (indicator = 'R')
      range_s  = S-sector grades  (indicator = 'S')

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8.  Sales Order Creation (on selection in ALV):
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

    On user action FCOD (Create), BAPI_SALESDOCU_CREATEFROMDATA is called:
      Doc Type  : ZP09 (Credit Memo Request)
      Condition : ZQIS at item level
      Quantity  : tot_elgl_qty
      Customer  : pkunag (from it_data_quater)

    After successful creation, record is logged in YRVA_REBATE table.
    Locking is done via ENQUEUE_EZ004_LOCK before creation.
