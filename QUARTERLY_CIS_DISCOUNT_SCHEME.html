Revised Criteria for Discount Applicability:

  • i. Revised: The Quarterly committed qty. (QCQ) shall now be revised to Bi-monthly committed Qty. (BCQ) i.e., Total (Jan'26MCQ + Feb'26MCQ) and slab shall be determined as {lifting of Jan + Lifting of Feb} /BCQ) x 100}.
  • ii. Additional Criteria: Slab Rate shall be determined as per above Sl.no. (1.i.) and shall be used to calculate discount on Mar'26 lifting. E.g. Discount calculation for Mar'26: (Slab rate as per sl.no. (1.i.) X CIS customer Mar'26 lifting).
  • iii. Revised: Discounts shall be disbursed on combined lifting of Jan'26 to Feb'26 & lifting of Mar'26 separately.
  • iv. Additional Criteria: In case, customer/s becomes eligible for higher discount slab as per original discount scheme i.e. {original % slab of QCQ (MCQ Jan'26+ MCQ Feb'26+MCQ Mar'26) and lifting (Jan'26+Feb'26+Mar'26)} as compared to eligibility as per revised Bi-monthly discount slabs then original discount scheme shall be applicable for those customers.
