Revised Criteria for Discount Applicability (Q4):

  • i. Revised: The Quarterly committed qty. (QCQ) shall now be revised to Bi-monthly committed Qty. (BCQ) i.e., Total (Jan MCQ + Feb MCQ) and slab shall be determined as {(Lifting of Jan + Lifting of Feb) / BCQ} × 100.
    Code reference: grp_lift_qty_m10 (Jan) + grp_lift_qty_m11 (Feb) compared against committed_qty_m10 + committed_qty_m11 (BCQ).
  • ii. Additional Criteria: Slab Rate shall be determined as per above Sl.no. (i.) and shall be used to calculate discount on Mar lifting. E.g. Discount calculation for Mar: (Slab rate as per sl.no. (i.)) × CIS customer Mar lifting.
    Code reference: ind_lift_qty_m12 (Mar) used as eligible qty for Mar component.
  • iii. Revised: Discounts shall be disbursed on combined lifting of Jan to Feb & lifting of Mar separately.
    Code reference: tot_elgl_qty = ind_elgl_qty_m1 (Jan) + ind_elgl_qty_m2 (Feb) + ind_elgl_qty_m3 (Mar).
  • iv. Additional Criteria: In case, customer/s becomes eligible for higher discount slab as per original discount scheme i.e. {original % slab of QCQ (MCQ Jan + MCQ Feb + MCQ Mar) and lifting (Jan+Feb+Mar)} as compared to eligibility as per revised Bi-monthly discount slabs then original discount scheme shall be applicable for those customers.
    Code reference: Customers with mou_begda ≥ 01-Feb-2024 are excluded from Q4 processing (lv_flag123 = 'X').
  • v. P&D Sector: Maximum percentage cap is replaced by pd_perc_q4 from master waiver table (YRVA_MSTR_WAIVER). If pd_perc_q4 is blank, no upper cap is applied (open-ended eligible qty).
  • vi. Waivers: Per-month waiver flags (YRVA_QAIS_ADD_WV) for JAN / FEB / MAR can override the minimum lifting eligibility check for individual months.

General Criteria for All Quarters (Q1, Q2, Q3):

  • i. Minimum Threshold: Group lifting must be ≥ Min% of QCQ (default 85%, stored in YRVA_MSTR_WAIVER - min_perc_qX). Below this, eligible quantity = 0.
  • ii. Maximum Cap: Group lifting beyond Max% of QCQ (default 125%, stored in YRVA_MSTR_WAIVER - max_perc_qX) is capped. Eligible qty = (ind_lift / grp_lift) × Max_Cap_Qty.
  • iii. Rate Determination: Rate (Rs./MT) fetched from SAP condition type ZQIS via A350 → KONM scale table. Highest scale bracket where kstbm ≤ customer's QCQ (mou_qty) is applied.
  • iv. Discount Amount: Value = Total Eligible Qty × Rate (kbetr). Credit Memo Request (doc type ZP09) is created via BAPI_SALESDOCU_CREATEFROMDATA and logged in YRVA_REBATE.
