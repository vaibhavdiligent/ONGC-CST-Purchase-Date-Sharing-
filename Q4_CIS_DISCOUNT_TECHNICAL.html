C. Revised Criteria for Discount Applicability

  • i. Revised BCQ (Bi-monthly Committed Qty):
    QCQ is revised to BCQ = Total (Jan'26MCQ + Feb'26MCQ). Slab is determined as {(Lifting of Jan + Lifting of Feb) / BCQ} x 100.
    BCQ = commited_qty_m10 + commited_qty_m11
    % achieved = (grp_lift_qty_m10 + grp_lift_qty_m11) / BCQ * 100
    Slab lookup: SORT i_cond DESCENDING BY kstbm
                 LOOP AT i_cond WHERE kstbm <= mou_qty → w_kbetr = kbetr

    Condition records fetched from: A350 (kschl='ZQIS', vkorg='5000') → KONM (knumh, kbetr, kstbm)
  • ii. Mar'26 Discount Calculation:
    Slab rate (determined in i.) is applied on Mar'26 individual lifting. (Slab rate as per sl.no. (1.i.) X CIS customer Mar'26 lifting).
    Mar eligible qty = ind_lift_qty_m12 (FORM q4_mar)
    value = tot_elgl_qty * w_kbetr
  • iii. Combined Disbursement:
    Discounts disbursed on combined lifting Jan'26 to Feb'26 & Mar'26 separately.
    tot_elgl_qty = ind_elgl_qty_m1 (Jan) + ind_elgl_qty_m2 (Feb) + ind_elgl_qty_m3 (Mar)
  • iv. Original Scheme Fallback:
    If customer is eligible for a higher slab under the original scheme {original % slab of QCQ (MCQ Jan'26 + MCQ Feb'26 + MCQ Mar'26) and lifting (Jan'26+Feb'26+Mar'26)} then original scheme applies.
    IF wa_yrva_qais_data-mou_begda GE '20240201'.

    ENDIF.
